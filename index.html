About Miso

Miso is a male cat who my partner and I adopted in 2019. He is a domestic short-hair tabby who we rescued via the RSPCA.

As a cat, naturally, he is both adorable and a little brat. He is very affectionate and loves cuddles and attention one second, but will then not want to be anywhere near you the next!
